About Our Program

This is a uniquely designed program that embraces the entrepreneurship mindset, specifically for students pursuing arts and humanities.

Artpreneurship is intended to create a broader understanding for artists and students in humanities to leverage their talent and develop a series of skills that will enhance their career path—whether in arts, dance, film, journalism, literature, music, radio, television, theatre, theology/religion, or writing—for business practices. Students in arts and humanities fields have always been known to “create,” and many do so for personal reasons and to share their work with others. Students combine creativity and critical thinking, and they are natural collaborators. Through Artpreneurship, we believe students in the program, while developing their personal work, can adapt and transform that work for an entrepreneurial venture.
